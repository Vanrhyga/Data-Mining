Gnovo Technologies Ltd. Profile
Gnovo Technologies is a high-tech company that specializes in computer aided design area. The principal focus of the Company is system level electronic design, CAD/CAE/CAM engineering, feasibility analysis, and IC design, etc. We are the distributor of a broad range of excellent products. 
The philosophy of our company is “integrity, collaboration and innovation”. We provide our clients with superior tools, outstanding technical support, as well as first class training and service. Our goal is to achieve success together with our clients.
Gnovo Technologies has established excellent relationship with companies in the diverse fields of space, aviation, electronics, shipping, weaponry, machinery, consumer electronics, and institutions of higher education, etc. Based in Beijing, the Company has its operating branch in Hong Kong.
